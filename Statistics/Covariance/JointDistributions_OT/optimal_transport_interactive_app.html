Optimal Transport: Kantorovich Plan Visualiser

Presets

Source Distribution (Top)

The starting pile of "dirt".

Target Distribution (Right)

Where the dirt needs to go.

Algorithm

Controls entropic regularisation. Lower = sharper transport plan (slower to converge). Higher = smoother, more diffuse plan.

Higher resolution gives smoother results but is slower.

How to read: The heatmap shows the optimal transport plan γ(x, y). Bright spots at (x, y) mean mass is moved from position x in the source to position y in the target. The marginals (side plots) show the source and target distributions.
